1D Chess

Welcome to 1D Chess! This variant of chess simplifies all the complexities of traditional chess into a single dimension. Challenge the AI as white and uncover acheving a victory may prove to be more challenging than anticipated. Can you find a forced checkmate for white?

Rules

The Rook can move any number of squares in any direction.

The Knight can move 2 squares in any direction jumping over pieces in the way.

The King can move one square in any direction.

You win the game if the opposition's king is attacked by one of your pieces and has no possible move to escape check.

A draw happens when:

  • There is no legal move for a player to play and they are not in check
  • There is not enough pieces to checkmate the other person
  • The same move is repeated three times in a game
